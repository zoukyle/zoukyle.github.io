Google Maps Business View

Google Maps Business View, which is Streetview for Business Interiors, is a premium-quality, 360-degree interactive virtual tour of your business powered by Street View technology. It's simple, fast and affordable.



Why get Google Business View?
  • Virtually "walk-in" your business to invite customers inside to experience your business ambiance and decor
  • Increase engagement with your business online
  • Stand out and differentiate your business from your competitors
  • Add premium/professional quality photography to your presence across Google
  • Make Google Search results, Google Maps, and Google+ of your business look special

FAQs:
Why Google Business View?
  • Google has created Business Views using the same "street view technology" that users have become accustomed to using. These tours capture multiple 360-degree panoramic photo shots of your business to give customers a better sense of what to expect when coming to your physical location. This technology brings your business to life, online, and will help to entice more potential customers.
How much does a tour cost?
  • Depending on the size of your business, the amount of floors you have and the amount of time we need to allocate to your business's Virtual Tour, prices may vary.
  • Usually between $200 and $750.
  • Please contact us for more information.
What do I get from the service?
  • 360 degree pano images published on Google+, Google Maps
  • Professional, high resolution POI (Point of Interest) static images, which include:
    • Storefront (wide angle shot of the entire front of the business including the entrance and the business name/sign)
    • Business name
    • Address (not suite number)
    • Wide angle shot(s) from the doorway looking into the business
    • Wide angle shot(s) from the back of the business looking toward the main entrance
    • Hours of operation
    • Close-up of business card
    • Type of payments
    • Close-up of menu (all pages) if applicable
How long does a tour take to photograph?
  • This is not the easiest question to answer. Typically most places take 1-2 hours to shot the tour, but every case is different.
How long will it take from my photo shoot to the tour being live on Google Maps?
  • After the photoshoot, generally it will take 5-10 business days to get the photo published on Google Maps.
  • This process can be delayed if Google QA ops find errors.
  • If there are errors, a second photo shoot may have to be scheduled. You do NOT need to pay for the reshoot.
How long can my Business View stay on Google's systems?
  • Forever until you request to take it down or reshoot
Is there a monthly fee to keep this service?
  • Nope
Which areas of my businesses will be photographed?
  • You can tell us exactly what you want to show off and what you do not want he public to see. Typically if it is an area in your store or business that customers, or clients see when they are in your business, we suggest making that a part of your tour.
  • Unorganized offices, storage areas and bathrooms can be left off of this tour, without a hesitation. Our Photographers can discuss these specifics before or during the shoot.
What time of day should we schedule our Virtual Tour photo shoot?
  • The best times are either before your store opens or after the business day. This way it doesn't disturb your normal work day, Photographers don't have to worry about blurring people's faces or being in the way.
What measures are in place to ensure privacy and legal compliance?
  • Our photographers will always alert customers and employees before a photo is taken, to uphold their privacy.
  • Google's protocol states that all faces must be blurred for the tour; with that in mind - the panoramic imagery will be processed by state-of-the-art blurring technology to blur out faces of any employees or customers who appear in the imagery.
Google is free, so why am I getting charged for Business View?
  • As a business owner, you will be paying the photographer or Agency and not Google
  • Not all Google products are free (ie. Google Apps for Business). However, Business View allows you to enhance your customer's experience. 
I'm not tech savvy. Is Business View for my business?
  • Although you may not be tech savvy, your customers are and may be looking for your business online
  • If you already have a business website, Business View is another great way for your customers to find you online
  • You don't need to be tech savvy. As a Business View Certified Agency, I can help you create your online presence
What's so great about Business View?
  • Business View gives you 360 degree pano shots of your business. You can virtually "walk-in" your business
  • Copyright of the photos are owned by the business owner, so the images can be used on your website, social media profiles, and any marketing purposes
  • Business View allows you to stand out and differentiate your business from your competitors
Can you give me some stats about this service?
  • There are more than one billion monthly active users of Google Maps
  • 1 in 5 searches on Google are related to location.
  • There are over 200K businesses that have Business View tours on Google.
  • Over 10K businesses are adopting Business View each month.
